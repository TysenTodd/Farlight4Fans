How many players can play in a match?
A match in Farlight 84 can have up to 40 players competing against each other.
While there is a 40 player cap, you can play with a full squad of your friends no matter your ranks.
What are the system requirements to play Farlight 84?
The minimum system requirements for Farlight 84 on PC include a Windows 10 operating system, an Intel Core i5 processor, 8GB of RAM, and a GeForce GTX 960 or AMD Radeon R9 280 graphics card.

If you are a IOS user, you must have IOS 12.0 or later installed, iPhone 6S or later, iPad Air 2 or later, iPad mini 4 or later, or iPod touch (7th generation) or later,
and at least 2 GB of available space.

Is there a ranked system in Farlight 84?
Yes, Farlight 84 features a ranking system that allows players to compete in ranked matches, climb the leaderboards, and earn rewards based on their performance.

There are 9 ranked tiers total: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Grandmaster, Ace, Legend, and Mythic.
